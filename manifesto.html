The Narrative First

I started out deeply immersed in the technical side of things, building digital systems and web architecture. But I eventually realized that technology is just a canvas.

Whether I am drafting a novel, conceptualizing a game, or planning an animation, my primary goal is to tell a compelling story. I simply use whatever tool allows me to bring those ideas into reality.

Bridging Systems and Art

Building a student organization, mapping out a game economy, or structuring a database all require a strong foundation. I love operating exactly where logic and creativity meet.

For me, it is about understanding how structural systems work, and then using that knowledge to create experiences that feel genuinely human and engaging.
